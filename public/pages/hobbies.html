Interests

Ever since the games 688 - attack sub, Gunship 2000, Doom, and Ladders came out I have loved being on computers.

When I found out that I could write games, I wrote versions of space invaders, tic tac toe (with AI player), break out (with level editor). Funny enough, I programmed these using the database language foxpro. The graphics were clunky, but I eventually was able to store my settings in a database which was nice, albeit slow.

I began my computer career at a media company as a data analyst. I used excel (programming macros) and foxpro (to generate queries and reports). From there, I was hired on with a company to run programs to massage data in preparation for the mailing within the postal service. I started writing application like an address label generating program.

When I am not glued to a computer monitor, I like to walk and enjoy nature. It could be just around the block or at a mesa 50 miles away.
